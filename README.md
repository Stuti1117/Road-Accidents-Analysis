# Road-Accidents-Analysis
# 🚗 Road Accident Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that analyzes road accident data to uncover patterns related to accident severity, driver demographics, vehicle types, and environmental conditions.

The goal is to help understand **key risk factors** and improve road safety insights.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

### 🔹 Key Focus Areas

* Accident Severity Analysis
* Driver Demographics (Age & Gender)
* Vehicle Movement & Type
* Weather & Road Conditions
* Driving Experience Impact

---

## 📷 Dashboard Features

### 🛣 Road Surface Analysis

* Majority of accidents occur on **asphalt roads**
* Other surfaces include gravel, earth, and mixed types

---

### 👨‍💼 Driver Demographics

* Analysis based on:

  * Gender (Male, Female, Unknown)
  * Age groups (Under 18, 18–30, 31–50, 50+)
* Majority of accidents involve **male drivers**

---

### 📅 Day of Week & Driving Experience

* Treemap visualization showing:

  * Accident distribution across weekdays
  * Driving experience categories (e.g., 1–2 yrs, 5–10 yrs)
* Helps identify **high-risk time periods**

---

### 🚙 Vehicle & Casualty Analysis

* Line chart showing:

  * Number of casualties by vehicle type
  * Severity levels:

    * Fatal Injury
    * Serious Injury
    * Slight Injury

---

### 🔄 Vehicle Movement Analysis

* Common movements analyzed:

  * Going straight
  * Turning
  * Reversing
  * Overtaking
* Provides insight into **accident-prone maneuvers**

---

### 🌦 Weather Conditions Impact

* Filters available:

  * Cloudy
  * Fog/Mist
  * Normal
  * Rainy
* Helps analyze how weather affects accident rates

## 📈 Insights Derived

* 📌 Most accidents occur on **asphalt roads**
* 📌 Male drivers are involved in majority of accidents
* 📌 Slight injuries are more common than fatal injuries
* 📌 Accidents frequently occur during **normal weather conditions**
* 📌 Certain driving movements (like going straight or turning) have higher accident rates
